Our story

With an experience of more than 10 year in salons and the developed expertise working with India's top beauty brands, the celebrity makeup and beauty artist and an entrepreneur Ms. Reema started her first outlet in Viman Nagar in Pune. Today Reeva comprise of the top-class professional designers and marquee artist in the hair, beauty and makeup industry. Reeva has become a brand to reckon with when it comes to out of the box gromming destination both for the male and the female. Its avant-garde, urban, current and innovative image and its excellent services have made Reeva the key signature brand that drives the business of aesthetics and beauty. Since its inception, it has chosen to be the benchmark of the sector; broke the traditional concept of hairdressing and revolutionized the panorama with its powerful brand image, unique decoration, the most uninterrupted schedule and the most complete list of services, products and quality treatments at a very competitive price. This new concept also integrated work processes, technology, business management, training and marketing with the sole objective of satisfying the customer and creating a profitable, secure and lasting relationship for the community.

History of the Company

Reeva was established 2 years ago with a single outlet at the center the happenning city of Pune at Viman Nagar. We entered into the hair dressing and beauty industry first hand with just one single motive, Customer Care and Reinventing Fashion in hairdressing. And since then, our core value transformed into a luxe fashion brand named Reeva. Our consistent quest and passion for inventing hairdressing has now become an iconic name and brand in the Hair and Beauty Industry of Pune serving millions of customers.

Design and Concept

The salon interiors are inspired from abstract and defined lines; giving the salon a very modern, plush and stylish look. The inspiration for the salon interiors comes mostly from the needs expressed by the growing awareness and more informed consumers.

While setting up the salon, we faced typical challenges that come from a very fast growing and radically changing industry. With the consumers being more informed and aware; we had to ensure that we live to the perception of ultimate in luxury yet affordable as per mindset of customers.

 

Corporate Vision

Our vision is to be a leading customer centric salon chain, warmly welcoming all customers for an experience which takes them on a high in terms of quality Hair, beauty & Skin services in a hygienic, relaxed, comfortable environment to make their day fabulous.

Customer Promise

Our commitment is to always try to master the art of exemplary services & experience; it is our endeavor to serve with excellence. We feel under oath to provide an unwinding environment with precision, detail and purpose where you can immerse yourself in peace and luxury.
We promise to deliver with consummate skill and impeccable ambience on every visit.
